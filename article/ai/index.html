What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn. AI is already impacting industries such as healthcare, transportation, education, and entertainment.

Applications of AI

  • Self-driving cars
  • Smart assistants (e.g., Siri, Alexa)
  • Medical diagnosis systems
  • Fraud detection
